Request Account and Data Deletion

This page explains how you can request the deletion of your account and associated data for Kolkata Bus-o-Pedia.

Steps to Request Data Deletion:

  1. Email us at kbopofficial@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request".
  2. Include your account details (e.g., registered email address) in the email.
  3. We will process your request and delete your data within 7–14 business days.

What Data Will Be Deleted:

  • Your name and email address.
  • Any associated app usage data.

Data Retention:

Some data (e.g., billing or transaction records) may be retained for legal or compliance purposes for up to 90 days.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at kbopofficial@gmail.com.
